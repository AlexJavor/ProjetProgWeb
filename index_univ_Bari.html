PRÉSENTATION

Politecnico di Bari is an Italian public scientific-technological university founded in 1990 and it is one of the leading institution for studying engineering and architecture in Italy. Basically located in Bari, that is the main city of Apulia Region, Politecnico has locations also in Foggia and Taranto.
