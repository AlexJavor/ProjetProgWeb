PRÉSENTATION

The University of Ljubljana implements and promotes basic, applied and developmental research and is pursuing excellence and the highest quality as well as the highest ethical criteria in all scientific fields and art. In these areas of national identity the University of Ljubljana specifically develops and promotes Slovenian scientific and professional terminology. With over 63,000 enrolled undergraduate and graduate students, it is among the largest universities in Europe.
